About Us

Rudasoft is a software product development firm led by Greg Rudakov and Stephen Rudakov. With over 20 years of expertise in software product strategy and delivery, Rudasoft specialises in translating business objectives into actionable technical strategies.

Our team has extensive experience working across industries including banking, education, public safety, manufacturing, and automotive, bringing a unique perspective to complex challenges. With a strong focus on B2B solutions, we excel at creating tailored strategies that align technical delivery with business goals. Rudasoft’s unique blend of product thinking and deep technical expertise ensures pragmatic and innovative solutions for your business needs.
